# Why Every CA Should Learn to Build AI Products

The Chartered Accountancy profession is at an inflection point. AI isn't coming for our jobs — it's coming for our outdated tools and manual processes.

## The Problem with Finance Tech

Most finance software was built in the pre-AI era. We're still using tools that require manual data entry, repetitive calculations, and hours of formatting. As CAs, we know the domain better than anyone — but we've been waiting for someone else to build the solutions.

## Why CAs Are Uniquely Positioned

Here's what most tech founders don't have: deep domain expertise in finance, tax law, and accounting standards. A CA who can code has a superpower — they understand both the problem and the technology to solve it.

## Getting Started

You don't need a CS degree. Start with Next.js for the frontend, learn to use the Claude API for AI capabilities, and use Supabase for your backend. Ship something small. Then iterate.

The future belongs to CAs who build.
